Popular questions

  • How do I change or cancel a booking?

    Contact the booking site (e.g., Booking.com) listed on your confirmation email. trivago can't modify partner bookings.

  • Why did the price change?

    Prices come directly from booking sites and can change at any time. Refresh results for the latest rate.

  • How do trivago ratings work?

    We aggregate reviews from multiple sources to surface a combined traveler score.
